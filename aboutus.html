About Nova

Nova is the world’s leading marketplace for beauty and wellness services, connecting millions of clients with top salons, spas, and professionals. Designed to simplify and enhance the booking experience, Nova offers an intuitive platform that allows businesses to manage appointments, payments, and customer relationships effortlessly. Our innovative software is completely subscription-free, providing businesses with powerful tools such as automated reminders, online payments, and marketing solutions to help them grow. Clients can browse, book, and enjoy a seamless beauty and wellness experience through Nova is easy-to-use app and website. With a commitment to innovation and excellence, Nova continues to redefine the beauty and wellness industry, making it more accessible, efficient, and rewarding for everyone.
